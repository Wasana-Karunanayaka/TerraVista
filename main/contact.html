Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how TerraVista handles your information when you use our contact form.

Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect the following information:

  • Full Name: To address you personally in our response.
  • Email Address: To reply to your inquiry.
  • Message: The content of your question or feedback.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose of responding to your message. We are a student project and do not store your data for marketing purposes, sell it to third parties, or use it for any other reason.

Data Security

As this is a demonstration website, please do not send any sensitive personal information through the contact form. All submissions are for project evaluation purposes only.

Your Consent

By using our contact form and checking the "I agree" box, you consent to this privacy policy.
