Must-Visit Destinations in Costa Rica

A paradise of microclimates, from misty cloud forests to sun-drenched coasts.

Manuel Antonio

A stunning national park where the rainforest meets white sand beaches. Famous for monkeys and sloths.

Arenal Volcano

A perfectly conical volcano surrounded by hot springs, waterfalls, and adventure activities in La Fortuna.

Monteverde

Explore the mystical high-altitude cloud forest, walk across hanging bridges, and spot the rare Quetzal bird.

Tortuguero

The "Amazon of Costa Rica," accessible only by boat/plane. A prime nesting site for Green Sea Turtles.

Corcovado

Located on the Osa Peninsula, this biologically intense park is home to tapirs, jaguars, and scarlet macaws.

Puerto Viejo

A laid-back Caribbean town with reggae vibes, surfing spots like Salsa Brava, and jaguar rescue centers.

Tamarindo

A top destination for surfing, nightlife, and beautiful sunsets on the Gold Coast of Guanacaste.

La Fortuna Waterfall

A breathtaking 70-meter drop into an emerald pool, surrounded by lush tropical jungle.

Tico Culture & Traditions

A peaceful nation that abolished its army to invest in education and nature.

🌴

Pura Vida

The national motto meaning "Pure Life." It's a greeting, a goodbye, and a philosophy of optimism and gratitude.

☕

Golden Bean (Coffee)

Costa Rica produces some of the world's best Arabica coffee. Coffee played a key role in the country's development.

🚜

Sarchí Oxcarts

Colorful, intricately painted oxcarts are a UNESCO masterpiece and a symbol of rural Costa Rican work ethic.

🍛

Gallo Pinto

The national breakfast dish of rice and black beans, fried together with spices and served with eggs and plantains.

🤳

Nicoya Blue Zone

The Nicoya Peninsula is one of five global "Blue Zones" where people live significantly longer, healthier lives.
