Must-Visit Destinations in the USA

A continent-sized country with everything from arctic tundra to tropical beaches.

Grand Canyon

Arizona. A natural wonder that defies description. Hike the Rim Trail or take a helicopter tour for expansive views.

New York City

The Big Apple. Visit the Statue of Liberty, catch a Broadway show, walk through Central Park, and see Times Square.

Yellowstone National Park

Wyoming. The world's first national park. Home to Old Faithful geyser, colorful hot springs, bison, and bears.

San Francisco

California. Famous for the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz Island, steep rolling hills, and historic cable cars.

Walt Disney World

Orlando, Florida. The Most Magical Place on Earth. Four theme parks including the Magic Kingdom and Epcot.

Las Vegas

Nevada. The Entertainment Capital of the World. Casinos, world-class shows, and neon lights on the famous Strip.

Hawaii

The Aloha State. Visit Volcanoes National Park on the Big Island to see active lava flows and lush rainforests.

Washington D.C.

The Capital. Walk the National Mall to see the Capitol, Lincoln Memorial, and the Smithsonian museums.

New Orleans

Louisiana. The Big Easy. Famous for Mardi Gras, jazz music on Frenchman Street, beignets, and Creole cuisine.

Yosemite National Park

California. An icon of the American West. Massive granite cliffs like El Capitan and Half Dome, plus giant sequoias.

American Culture

A melting pot of traditions, driven by innovation and individualism.

💵

Tipping Culture

It is not optional. Waiters rely on tips. Standard is 15-20% for service. Also tip bartenders and taxi drivers.

🎥

Hollywood

The global center of film and television. American pop culture exports shape the world's entertainment landscape.

🚗

Road Trips

Route 66. The freedom of the open road is a core American value. Interstate highways connect coast to coast.

🏈

American Sports

Football (NFL), Baseball (MLB), and Basketball (NBA). The Super Bowl is an unofficial national holiday.

🎷

Jazz & Blues

Born in the South (New Orleans, Memphis). Truly original American art forms that gave birth to Rock 'n' Roll.
