Must-Visit Destinations in Aotearoa

A land of long white clouds, dramatic landscapes, and friendly hobbits.

Milford Sound

The "Eighth Wonder of the World." Towering peaks, cascading waterfalls, and deep dark waters in Fiordland.

Hobbiton Movie Set

The Shire comes to life. Walk through the lush pastures of the Shire, visit Bag End, and have a drink at the Green Dragon.

Queenstown

The Adventure Capital of the World. Bungee jumping, jet boating, skiing, and stunning views of Lake Wakatipu.

Rotorua

A geothermal wonderland of bubbling mud pools, shooting geysers (Pohutu), and rich Maori culture.

Waitomo Caves

A magical underground boat ride under a starry galaxy of thousands of bioluminescent glowworms.

Aoraki / Mount Cook

New Zealand's highest peak. A rugged alpine playground with glaciers, turquoise lakes, and starry attractions.

Franz Josef Glacier

One of the steepest glaciers in New Zealand, descending from the Southern Alps into rainforest near sea level.

Tongariro Crossing

Often called the world's best day hike. traverse volcanic craters, emerald lakes, and "Mount Doom" (Ngauruhoe).

Bay of Islands

A subtropical micro-region known for its stunning beauty, history (Waitangi), sailing, and big-game fishing.

Māori Heritage & Kiwi Life

"Manaakitanga" — A traditional Maori value of hospitality and kindness.

💪

The Haka

A traditional war dance made famous by the All Blacks. It's a fierce display of strength, unity, and tribal pride.

🍗

Hāngī

A traditional feast cooked in an earth oven using heated rocks. Earthy, smoky flavors of meat and kumara.

🏈

Rugby Union

The national obsession. The All Blacks are legendary, and match days see the whole country come to a standstill.

🦗

The Kiwi

An odd, flightless, nocturnal bird that serves as the national symbol. New Zealanders proudly call themselves "Kiwis."

🏠

The Marae

The sacred meeting ground of Mäori communities. Visitors can experience a 'Powhiri' (welcome ceremony) here.
