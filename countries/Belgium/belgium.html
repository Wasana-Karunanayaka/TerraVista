Must-Visit Destinations in Belgium

From the capital of the EU to fairy-tale medieval towns.

Brussels

The capital, home to the stunning Grand Place, the Atomium, and the Manneken Pis.

Bruges

The "Venice of the North," a medieval fairy-tale town with winding canals and cobbled streets.

Ghent

A vibrant university city known for its medieval architecture, Gravensteen castle, and quirky vibe.

Antwerp

The world's diamond capital and a hub of fashion, art (Peter Paul Rubens), and cool cafes.

The Ardennes

A region of extensive forests, rough terrain, and rolling hills, perfect for hiking and history (WWII).

Dinant

A picturesque cliff-side town on the Meuse River, birthplace of Adolphe Sax, inventor of the saxophone.

Culture & Delicacies

Belgium is famous for its fine tastes, quirky humor, and artistic heritage.
