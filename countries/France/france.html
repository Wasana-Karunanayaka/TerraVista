Must-Visit Destinations in France

Beyond the lights of Paris, discover a diverse landscape of history, coastlines, and vineyards.

Eiffel Tower

The global icon of France. Ascend for breathtaking views of Paris, especially dazzling at night.

The Louvre

The world's largest art museum, home to the Mona Lisa and thousands of masterworks.

Palace of Versailles

The opulent royal château famed for its Hall of Mirrors and magnificent endless gardens.

Mont Saint-Michel

A magical island commune in Normandy topped by a gravity-defying medieval monastery.

French Riviera

The glamorous Mediterranean coastline known for Nice, Cannes, and azimuth blue waters.

Loire Valley

Known as the "Garden of France," featuring abundance of vineyards and fairy-tale châteaux.

Provence

Famous for its lavender fields, ancient Roman ruins, and sun-drenched landscapes.

Culture & Lifestyle

From high fashion to the slow enjoyment of a meal, embrace the French "Joie de vivre".

🧀

Gastronomy

French cuisine is UNESCO Heritage status. Wine and cheese are serious business, and meals are long, social affairs.

☕

Café Culture

People-watching from a terrace while sipping espresso is a national pastime and hub of social life.

👠

Haute Couture

France is the fashion capital of the world. Elegance and style are ingrained in daily life.

🎨

Art & Heritage

Birthplace of Impressionism and home to countless monuments. Culture is publicly funded and cherished.

🤝

"La Bise"

The traditional greeting of kissing on both cheeks. Always say "Bonjour" when entering a shop.

French Phrasebook

Politeness is key in France. Start every interaction with "Bonjour".
