Must-Visit Destinations in Switzerland

A playground of mountains, lakes, and charming medieval towns.

The Matterhorn

Zermatt. The world's most photographed mountain. A jagged pyramid soaring 4,478 meters into the sky.

Jungfraujoch

Top of Europe. The highest railway station in Europe, offering breathtaking views of the Aletsch Glacier.

Lake Geneva

Europe's largest Alpine lake. Home to the Jet d'Eau, the vineyards of Lavaux, and the jazz city of Montreux.

Lucerne

A picture-perfect city. Famous for the covered Kapellbrücke (Chapel Bridge), the Lion Monument, and Lake Lucerne.

Interlaken

"Between Lakes." The adventure capital of Switzerland. Skydiving, canyoning, and the gateway to the Bernese Oberland.

Château de Chillon

Montreux. An island castle on Lake Geneva made famous by Lord Byron. The most visited historic building in the country.

Rhine Falls

Europe's most powerful waterfall. Feel the roar of the water from a boat ride near Schaffhausen.

Bern

The capital. Its medieval Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage site, featuring 6km of arcades and the Zytglogge clock tower.

Zurich

A global banking hub with a creative heart. Enjoy the trendy Zurich West, the beautiful lakefront, and high-end shopping.

Grindelwald

A village at the base of the Eiger North Face. Stunning scenery in both winter (skiing) and summer (hiking).

Swiss Excellence

Precision, privacy, punctuality, and pure quality.

⌚

Watchmaking

Swiss Made. A symbol of quality and precision. From Swatch to Rolex, horology is in the national DNA.

🍫

Swiss Chocolate

The inventors of milk chocolate (Daniel Peter). Lindt, Toblerone, and countless artisanal chocolatiers.

🧀

Cheese Fondue

Melted Gruyère and Emmental with white wine and garlic. Dipping bread is a communal winter tradition.

🚆

Punctuality

If the train is one minute late, people check their watches. The transport system is famously efficient.

🎶

Alpine Tradition

The sounds of the mountains. Yodeling was once a way to communicate across valleys; Alphorns are massive wooden wonders.
