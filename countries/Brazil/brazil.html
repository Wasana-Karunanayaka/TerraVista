Must-Visit Destinations in Brazil

A continental country of immense diversity, from colonial towns to wild rainforests.

Christ the Redeemer

Rio de Janeiro. The iconic Art Deco statue atop Mount Corcovado, offering panoramic views of the Marvelous City.

Iguazu Falls

One of the New Seven Wonders of Nature. A massive system of 275 waterfalls straddling the border with Argentina.

Amazon Rainforest

The "Lungs of the Earth." Explore the river from Manaus, stay in a jungle lodge, and spot pink dolphins.

Sugarloaf Mountain

Take the cable car up this granite peak at the mouth of Guanabara Bay for the best sunset in Rio.

Copacabana & Ipanema

The world's most famous city beaches. Play volleyball, drink coconut water, and watch the world go by.

Salvador

The Afro-Brazilian capital. The historic Pelourinho district is filled with colorful colonial houses and drumming.

Pantanal

The world's largest tropical wetland. The best place in the Americas to spot wildlife like jaguars, caimans, and macaws.

Lençóis Maranhenses

A surreal landscape of vast white sand dunes filled with crystal-clear rainwater lagoons.

Fernando de Noronha

An exclusive volcanic archipelago. Known for its undeveloped beaches and incredible scuba diving.

Brasilia

The futuristic capital designed by Oscar Niemeyer. A UNESCO site famous for its modernist architecture.

The Rhythm of Brazil

Life is lived outside, with music, dance, and passion.

🎉

Carnival

The greatest show on Earth. A week of samba parades, street parties (blocos), and costumes.

🎶

Samba & Bossa Nova

Music is the soul of Brazil. From the energetic drums of Samba to the smooth jazz of Bossa Nova ("Girl from Ipanema").

⚽

Futebol

Not just a sport, but a religion. Brazil is home to Pelé and the most successful national team in history.

🥂

Capoeira

A unique Afro-Brazilian martial art that combines dance, acrobatics, and music.

🥩

Churrasco

Brazilian BBQ. Juicy cuts of meat (picanha) skewered and grilled over charcoal, often served "Rodizio" style.
